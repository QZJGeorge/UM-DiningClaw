Today's Pick · 2026-04-29
Mosher-Jordan's Mongolian Beef with Fried Noodles takes the crown tonight — or swing by Bursley for that irresistible Lamb Gyro Meat! 🥩
Head to Mosher-Jordan tonight if you're after quality meat! The star of the show is the Mongolian Beef with Fried Noodles — savory, saucy, and loaded with umami flavor. Pair that with the Vernor's Pound Cake for dessert and you've got a seriously solid dinner. Bursley is a close second with their Lamb Gyro Meat on the Halal station — tender, herby lamb is always a win. South Quad also brings the Thai Style Fried Rice with Shrimp and Mongolian Beef if you want options.
